About KW Controls

Who We Are

KW Controls provides industrial automation engineering services focused on Rockwell Automation, Siemens, and other leading industrial control platforms.

With over 10 years of experience, we have supported automation projects across a wide range of industries, including:

  • Pharmaceutical & Biotechnology Clean Utilities
  • Industrial Water & Wastewater Treatment
  • HVAC & Refrigeration Systems
  • Chemical & Biomass Processing
  • Material Handling & Conveyor Systems
  • Ports & Terminal Automation
  • Strapping Machinery

Our Mission

KW Controls delivers reliable, scalable, and maintainable process automation and system integration solutions that maximize productivity, enhance system reliability, and minimize downtime.
