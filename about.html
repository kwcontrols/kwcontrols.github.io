About KW Controls

Who We Are

KW Controls provides industrial automation engineering services focused on Rockwell Automation, Siemens, and other leading industrial control platforms.

With over 20 years of experience, we have supported automation projects across a wide range of industries, including:

  • Pharmaceutical & Biotechnology Clean Utilities (WFI, CIP/SIP, Pure Steam)
  • Industrial Water & Wastewater Treatment
  • HVAC & Refrigeration Systems
  • Chemical & Biomass Processing
  • Oil & Gas Facilities
  • Material Handling & Conveyor Systems
  • Ports & Terminal Automation
  • Strapping & Packaging Machinery

Our Mission

Whether commissioning a new production line, upgrading a legacy PLC system, or providing troubleshooting support, KW Controls delivers reliable, maintainable automation solutions that improve productivity and minimize downtime.
