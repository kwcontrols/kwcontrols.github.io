Welcome to KW Controls

With over 10 years of experience in the industry, KW Controls provides professional industrial automation services throughout Canada and the United States. We specialize in Rockwell Automation PLC programming, FactoryTalk HMI development, system troubleshooting, commissioning, and automation upgrades.

Our Services

PLC Programming

PLC software development or rewrite for modular, scalable and maintainable industrial automation systems.

SCADA/HMI Development

HMI servers and clients, historian, operator interface, OPC, alarm systems, recipes, datalogs, trends, reports and diagnostics.

Commissioning

Machine startup, I/O checkout, production support, and FAT/SAT assistance.

Troubleshooting

Fast diagnosis of PLC, HMI, VFD, networking, instruments, and production downtime.

Electrical Design and Automation Upgrades

PLC migration, system retrofit, electrical schematics and panel layouts.

Remote Support

Remote troubleshooting, software modifications, and emergency production support.
